Dive Site

  • Gale Reef

    This is a dive site best accessible from Colombo and Mount Lavina. Gale Reef South is flat ground site with rows and rows of Gorgonian Fans. Dharshana Jayawardena says, ‘never before have laid eyes on such a vast profusion of small and large Gorgonian Fans anywhere in Sri Lanka’. Strong currents is expected. And rapid head down descent may required. Our dive master does not use anchors on this site. Redtooth Triggerfish are spotted.

  • Dawson’s Reef

    Dawson Reef is a reef(s) and is located in Milne Bay, Papua New Guinea. The estimate terrain elevation above seal level is 9 metres.
