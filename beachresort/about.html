About

WHERE LOCATED

Conveniently located a mere 1-hour away from Gurgaon, Heritage Village Resort & Spa, Manesar, Gurgaon is a pet friendly Rajasthani themed resort destination. Surrounded by vast acres of lush greenery the hotel boasts Rajasthani haveli-like architecture framed in picturesque backdrops, and promises to deliver service excellence for relaxing holidays, rejuvenating wellness retreats, seamless conferences, and fairy-tale weddings making it ideal for a city escape or a quick getaway

Heritage Village Resort & Spa, Manesar, Gurgaon is conveniently located on NH 8, right off the Delhi- Jaipur Highway ( NH 8) at a 30 minutes’ drive from New Delhi’s International Airport and a 20-minute drive from Gurgaon’s business district

DETAILS

Surrounded by greenery, this refined, resort spa hotel is 9 km from Garhi Harsaru Junction Railway Station and 17 km from Gurgaon's city centre. The elegant rooms and suites with wood accents feature Wi-Fi, flat-screens and minibars, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add parquet floors and sitting areas with sofas, as well as additional flat-screen TVs. Upgraded, ornate suites add marble floors, 4-poster beds, private indoor splash pools and bathrooms with flat-screen TVs. There are 3 restaurants, a bar, a tea lounge, and a spa with Ayurvedic treatments. Other amenities include an outdoor pool, a fitness room and a business centre.
